Here’s how we got started…

We started our culinary and oenology experiences as most do with friends and get togethers at college, however it soon progressed into a career of sourcing and sampling many of the great products for a trendy little grocer in Southern California. Literally, travelling the world, finding the best in cheese and wine artistry was our passion that turned into our livelihood. Soon after we expanded on our love of food and drink by opening a fun, food centric Irish pub.

Sharing our food and wine experiences and knowledge is our passion. Sourcing great cheeses from around the world is challenging, yet it is made easier by already having met some of the same cheese artisans from previous travels.

Pairing these fine cheeses with fabulous wines makes the journey even more fun. We find the best wines to make the experiences of our patrons memorable.

Tasting Results

Beaulieu Vineyards

9 Time Winner

Beaulieu Vineyard is a vineyard near Rutherford, California, belonging to the appellation Rutherford AVA. It was established by Georges de Latour and his wife Fernande in 1900. This wine pairs beautifully with a brie cheese after dinner.

Lazy Creekside Vineyards

4 Time Winner

Creekside Vineyards Winery & Tasting Room rests south of the Quad Cities on historic Highway 67. This 1938 building has offered travelers a stopover of good conversation and extraordinary, artisan wines for the last several years. This wine is enjoyed accompanied with gruyere cheese.

Tula Vista

6 Time Winner

“A favorite wine to pair with lamb and beef. Has aged extremely well and is ready to drink yesterday. Calling all wine aficionados to enjoy this rare vintage.” Serve with extra sharp cheddar.
